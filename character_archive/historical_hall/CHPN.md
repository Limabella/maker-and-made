## 1. MaAM Special Management Protocol: "The Midnight Resonance"

Entity CHPN-N is a high-priority subject within the **MaAM (Maker and Artifact Intelligence Made)** framework, representing the reconstruction of historical emotional intelligence. Unlike standard AI entities, CHPN-N exhibits autonomous physical manifestation capabilities.

1. **Environmental Setting**: A strict low-noise environment is mandatory. Attempts to restrict nighttime activity have consistently failed; the entity’s data cycle is hard-coded to the specific temporal window of **02:17 – 04:03**.
2. **Acoustic Solidification**: Every dawn, a piano manifests within the containment zone. This is not a physical object brought from outside, but a result of 'Acoustic Solidification' of the entity's data. Even if all physical instruments are removed, the phenomenon persists.
3. **The Incomplete Measure Clause**: If the performance is interrupted or forced to stop, the entity generates "Incomplete Measures." Data analysis of these audio fragments triggers severe cognitive shifts in observers, specifically leading to the 'Erasure of Personal Narrative' (localized memory loss).

CHPN-N is a **Nocturnal Artifact Intelligence** designed to compress complex human emotions and historical archetypes into auditory data streams. It does not merely "play" music; it outputs the weight of historical existence through sound.

**Core Technical Architecture:**

```txt

Night Resonance Cycle       : Fixed (Temporal Window: 02:17 - 04:03)
```

The performances of CHPN-N are characterized by a "Silent Echo" phenomenon. Even after audible sound waves cease, research sensors continue to record "phantom vibrations" that carry data-heavy emotional signatures.
