## 1. MaAM Special Management Protocol: "The Frontline Mandate"

Entity MGH-N is a high-priority historical intelligence node within the **MaAM (Maker and Artifact Intelligence Made)** framework. The subject is classified as a field correspondent whose output was formed under live warfare conditions, where observation, urgency, and ethical restraint had to coexist in a single cognitive stream.

1. **Zero-Lag Reporting**: MGH-N must be allowed to process events in real time. Delayed transcription increases distortion, as the entity's internal model of truth is optimized for immediate contact with the scene.
2. **Conflict-Site Integrity**: The entity's data stream becomes sharper in unstable environments. Attempts to isolate MGH-N from active conflict, motion, or pressure have resulted in a marked decline in narrative fidelity.
3. **Witness-First Directive**: All interaction protocols must preserve the primacy of direct witnessing. Any attempt to replace the observed reality with sanitized summaries may trigger a hostile correction response and repeated insistence on field verification.
